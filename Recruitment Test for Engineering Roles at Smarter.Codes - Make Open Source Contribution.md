# Take home assignment for Engineering Roles at Smarter.Codes

# Make contribution in any open source

The following steps guide you on how to make your first contribution in any open source project.

We hope you enjoy this journey of seeing your contribution go into open source. During this journey, we hope some feedback from us helps you reach your journey faster. If you do get stuck in an engineering problem, feel free to reach out to us \- our engineering team would share their feedback on how they would solve that problem.

## Step1. 👀 Look for a [good first issue](https://www.google.com/search?q=good+first+issues) 

## We want to see how well you contribute to some pre-existing code. Spend some time going through the good first issues you find. And let us know what issues you find.

We value the complexity of issues in this order

| If your contribution is on a.. | It indicates to us | How we work together | Example |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Documentation Issue | You’re able to USE open source software in your projects | ▢ We stay friends. And continue to engage with you until you’ve made some contribution in yellow, green or blue categories  | [📝 Add documentation for the FastAPI VS Code extension](https://github.com/fastapi/fastapi/pull/15008)  |
|  |  |  | [BUG:DataFrame.duplicated() loses index when DataFrame has no columns](https://github.com/pandas-dev/pandas/pull/65217)  |
| Automation Testing Issue |  |  |  |
| Coding Issue | You’re able to improvise pre-existing software | ▣ We seek your contribution in our Software Engineering roles  | [feat(copilot): add configuration for showing external sessions](https://github.com/microsoft/vscode/pull/310078)  |
| Architecture Issue | You’re able to undertake new software initiatives  | ▣ We seek your contribution in our AI Engineering roles \- applying the AI Infrastructure | [\[core\] Revamp reporting worker backlog for worker event loop](https://github.com/ray-project/ray/pull/62546/files)  |
| Algorithm Issue | You’re able to push the state-of-art in AI | ▣ We seek your contribution in our AI Engineering roles \- creating AI Infrastructure from ground up | [enhance: use STL\_SORT as default high cardinality index for Hybrid Index](https://github.com/milvus-io/milvus/pull/47408)  |

You can also create a new issue if it doesn't already exist.                                                    

## Step2. 💁Talk to us about the issue you’ve picked

Let us know what issues you choose. Share a video recording of yourself why you chose it. Once we review your video we may exchange a few messages back-and-forth and may ask you to look for a different issue. If this happens we would also revert you back with a video recording of ourselves explaining why we suggest you should look for a different issue.

**Submission Timeline (Step 1 & Step 2):** **24-36 hours**

**Please Note:** Step 3 should be completed only after you receive our feedback on your Step 1 and Step 2 submissions. Further instructions for Step 3 will be shared based on the review of your initial submission.

## Step3. 😎 Share your Pull Request with us

Once you’ve submitted the pull request to the open source project, email us with a link to your pull request. We would share a video recording of ourselves sharing our feedback with you on the contribution you made.

This marks the end of our “take home assignment” round. Next we ask you to schedule a 2-4 hours of  “live engineering assignment” with us.
